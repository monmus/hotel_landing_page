​Welcome to our unique boutique bed & breakfast with a quiet garden, located in the centre of Tatras.​​​
​

​Villa Park is a chalet villa built in 1897, recently renovated and furnished with an eclectic mix of antique, modern and contemporary furniture and artworks, but still breathing the charm of the early 20th century.​​
​

OUR ROOMS

The holiday home comes with cosy bedrooms, two beautiful verandas, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a fully equipped kitchen that provides guests with a dishwasher, a microwave, a washing machine, a fridge and an oven.

We have several individually designed double and single rooms and you are welcome to use the three ground floor parlors, equipped with cosy sofas, armchairs, a writing desk and tables. Cuddle up in front of the open fireplace or use the library to swap a book.​​ ​There are also two beautiful huge verandas, a covered terrace and the wild romantic garden, in which you may roam.​​

ECLECTIC, CHARMING, COSY

Staff at the holiday home are always available to provide guidance at the reception.

AROUND TOWN

Located in Bukowina, close to Zakopane, Villa Park, Ski-in Ski-out at Tatras Highlands provides accommodation with restaurant, ski equipment hire, ski-to-door access, a fitness centre and a shared lounge. Featuring a patio, the holiday home is in an area where guests can engage in activities such as cycling, fishing and billiards.
